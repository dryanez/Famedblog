## Mistake #8: Memorizing Without Understanding üß†

### The Problem

**Example:** Memorizing "Give aspirin for MI" without understanding why, when, contraindications, or dose

### The Solution
‚úÖ Understand WHY, not just WHAT  
‚úÖ Learn pathophysiology before management  
‚úÖ Practice clinical reasoning  
‚úÖ Explain concepts to study partners

**Active Learning Techniques:**
