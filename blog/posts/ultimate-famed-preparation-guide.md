## Exam Day Strategy

### Before the Exam

- **Sleep well** - 7-8 hours the night before
- **Eat breakfast** - Light, protein-rich meal
- **Arrive early** - 30 minutes before start time
- **Bring ID** - Passport or Personalausweis
- **Stay calm** - You've prepared well

### During the Exam

1. **Read carefully** - Every word matters in German
2. **Manage time** - Don't spend >2 min per question
3. **Answer all questions** - No penalty for wrong answers
4. **Mark difficult ones** - Come back if time permits
5. **Trust your preparation** - Don't second-guess too much

### Question-Answering Technique

1. Read the question stem carefully
2. Identify key information
3. Formulate your own answer
4. Look for matching option
5. Eliminate obviously wrong answers
6. Choose the best remaining option
